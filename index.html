Virtual & In-Person Classes and Performances

DANCE CLASSES

We offer Odissi dance lessons in the tradition of Guru Pankaj Charan Das. Classes for adults & kids at all levels -- in Seattle, Bothell, and Bellevue, WA.

PERFORMANCES

Urvasi Ensemble performs Odissi classical dance locally, nationally, and internationally. Check out our upcoming and past performances!

Odissi History

Odissi is a classical dance that has its roots in the yogic temple rites of the priestesses of ancient Orissa or Kalinga. The dance was nearly lost during British rule.

Urvasi Ensemble Gallery

Urvasi Dancers, founded and headed by Dr. Ratna Roy, are an artistic treasure of the Pacific Northwest. Their Odissi dance performances are a sheer joy and a pleasing spectacle, demonstrating serious adherence to tradition in dance, costume and music, interesting repertoire, and creative choreography.

-- Latha Sambamurti, Washington State Arts Commissioner

Ratna Roy is a dancer with a difference. She has a conscience alive to feminist causes and endeavours to do her bit.

-- Leela Venkataraman

Urvasi teachers make the movements and stories of Odissi come to life for people from all backgrounds. I had no prior experience with Indian dance,but their classes and performances opened a magical door to the art of "yogic dance".

--Margot Miller, student
